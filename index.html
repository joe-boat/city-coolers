FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about our services. Still have questions? Ask our AI assistant.

In Ghana's hot, humid coastal climate, we strongly recommend servicing your AC every 3–4 months rather than the 6-month cycle typical in cooler countries. The combination of high humidity, dust, and continuous heavy usage accelerates filter clogging, coil fouling, and refrigerant issues. Regular servicing extends system life by 40–60% and keeps energy consumption optimal.
We install and service all major brands available in Ghana — including Daikin, LG, Samsung, Carrier, Gree, Midea, Haier, TCL, Hisense, and Panasonic. For new installations, we recommend Daikin or LG for their reliability in tropical climates and the availability of spare parts in Ghana. We'll always recommend what's genuinely best for your situation and budget.
Installation costs depend on AC type, unit size, and the complexity of the installation (pipe run length, wall type, height). For a typical 1–1.5 ton residential split unit, total installed cost in Takoradi currently ranges from GH₵ 3,500–GH₵ 7,500 depending on the brand and specification. Commercial and cassette units are priced per survey. We provide free site surveys and fully itemised quotes with no obligation.
Yes — we provide emergency response for critical system failures, particularly for commercial facilities, cold rooms, and server rooms where downtime causes significant losses. Call or WhatsApp us on 054 699 9753 and we'll dispatch the nearest available engineer. We aim to respond within 2–4 hours for emergencies across Takoradi and Sekondi.
All installation work carries a 2-year workmanship warranty. This means if any installation fault develops within 2 years, we return and rectify it at no charge. Equipment manufacturer warranties (typically 1–5 years depending on brand) are handled separately. We register your warranty on your behalf and assist with any manufacturer warranty claims throughout the coverage period.
